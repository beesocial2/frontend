Who we are?

Bee Social is blockchain platform that connects volunteers, commercial companies, non-profit organizations (NPO) to increase collaboration and maximize social impact. We want to create together with our community the biggest force for doing good in the world!

More details!

What honeycombs mean?

This is social tokens of our platform, which exist on blockchain Golos for transparent and safe transactions. Each resource, placed in section Fund of Resources, has its own value in honeycombs. You can earn and spend these honeycombs only on our platform Bee Social.

How can I earn honeycombs?

You can earn honeycombs through volunteer work in organizations, start-ups, initiatives, helping them to realize their social ideas.

How I can spend honeycombs?

Honeycombs you can spend if you want to get resources from section Fund of Resources, or you can transfer honeycombs to Social Organizations that you want to help.
